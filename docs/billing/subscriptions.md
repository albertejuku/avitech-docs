# Subscriptions

## Viewing Your Current Plan

Your active plan is shown on the **Dashboard** under **My Subscription**.

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Subscriptions → Change Plan**
2. Select a higher-tier plan
3. Confirm the upgrade
4. The new plan takes effect immediately and is prorated

## Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
