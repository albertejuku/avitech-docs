# Billing Troubleshooting

## Payment Not Reflecting

- Wait 5–10 minutes and refresh
- Check your Mobile Money confirmation message for the transaction ID
- Contact support with the transaction ID if still not showing

## Cannot Log In

- Ensure your account number/email is correct
- Try resetting your password
- Contact support if your account may be suspended

## Wrong Amount Charged

Contact support immediately with your invoice number. Do not make another payment until resolved.
