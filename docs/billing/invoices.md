# Invoices & Receipts

## Downloading an Invoice

1. Go to **Billing History**
2. Find the invoice you need
3. Click the **Download PDF** button

## Understanding Your Invoice

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Invoice No. | Unique reference number |
| Due Date | Payment must be made by this date |
| Service Period | The month this invoice covers |
| Amount Due | Total payable |
