# Your Account

## Profile

Go to the user menu in the top-right corner and select **Profile** to manage your account.

### Update Your Details

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| Full name | Displayed throughout the portal |
| Email | Used for login and notifications |
| Phone number | Used for SMS notifications and two-factor authentication |
| Profile picture | Shown in the top bar and in tickets |

### Change Password

1. Go to **Settings → Security**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password
4. Click **Save Changes**

!!! warning "Password requirements"
    Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

## Notification Preferences

Control which emails and SMS messages you receive:

- Go to **Settings → Notifications**
- Toggle on/off: payment alerts, ticket assignments, subscriber status changes, system announcements

## Sign Out

Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **Sign Out** to end your session.
