# Making Payments

## Payment Methods Accepted

- Mobile Money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money)
- Visa / Mastercard
- Bank Transfer

## How to Pay

1. Log in to the billing portal
2. Click **Pay Now** on your dashboard
3. Select your payment method
4. Enter the amount and confirm
5. You will receive a confirmation SMS and email

!!! warning
    Payments may take up to 5 minutes to reflect on your account.
