# Revenue Metrics

The top of the Subscriptions page shows six revenue metric cards that give you a real-time financial snapshot of your business.

## Metric Cards

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| MRR | **Monthly Recurring Revenue** — the total value of all active subscriptions normalized to a monthly figure. The active subscriber count is shown beneath |
| ARR | **Annual Recurring Revenue** — MRR × 12. Represents the annualized value of your current subscriber base |
| Month-to-Date | Revenue collected in the current calendar month. Includes a **Month-over-Month %** change indicator showing growth or decline vs. the same period last month |
| Last Month | Total revenue collected in the previous complete calendar month. Includes a **Year-over-Year %** change vs. the same month last year |
| Year-to-Date | Revenue collected since January 1 of the current year. Includes a **YTD %** change vs. the same period last year |
| Today | Revenue collected today. Includes a **Day-over-Day %** change vs. yesterday |

## Understanding the Metrics

**MRR vs MTD:**

- **MRR** is a forecast — it tells you what you *should* collect monthly if all current subscribers renew
- **MTD** is actuals — it tells you what you *have* collected so far this month

If MTD is significantly lower than MRR, it may indicate that renewals are behind or that a significant number of subscribers are expiring without payment.

**Percentage Change Indicators:**

- **Green upward arrow** — positive growth vs. the comparison period
- **Red downward arrow** — decline vs. the comparison period

These are calculated automatically from your payment and subscription data. No manual input is required.

## Using Metrics for Business Decisions

| Metric | How to Use |
|---|---|
| MRR | Track subscriber base growth month over month |
| ARR | Use for annual business planning and forecasting |
| MTD vs MRR gap | Identify collection shortfalls early in the month |
| YoY comparisons | Measure true business growth excluding seasonal patterns |
| Today's revenue | Monitor daily cash flow and payment activity |

!!! tip
    Check the **Expiring (7 days)** filter on the subscriptions list alongside MRR to proactively contact subscribers who need to renew — preventing a drop in next month's MTD.
