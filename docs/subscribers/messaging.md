# Sending Messages to Subscribers

You can send an SMS or email directly to any subscriber from the subscriber list or from their detail page.

## Sending to a Single Subscriber

1. On the **Subscriber List**, click the actions menu ( ⋮ ) next to the subscriber
2. Click **Send Message**
3. Choose the channel: **SMS** or **Email**
4. For Email, enter a **Subject**
5. Type your message in the body
6. Click **Send**

!!! note
    The message is sent to the subscriber's registered phone number (SMS) or email address. If either is missing from their profile, that channel will be unavailable.

## Sending to Multiple Subscribers (Broadcast)

For bulk messaging, go to the **Subscribers → Communications** section in the sidebar. This allows you to:

- Filter your audience by status, zone, router, or service type
- Preview how many subscribers will receive the message
- Send SMS or email to the entire filtered group

### Tips for Bulk Messaging

- Use status filter **Suspended** to notify subscribers with overdue accounts
- Use status filter **Expiring (7 days)** to send renewal reminders
- Always preview the recipient count before sending to avoid unintended mass messages
