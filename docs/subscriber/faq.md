# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I pay my bill?
Log in to the billing portal, click **Pay Now**, and follow the prompts. We accept Mobile Money, card, and bank transfer.

## My internet is slow — what do I do?
Restart your router first. If the problem continues, check the network status page or call support.

## I forgot my password
Click **Forgot Password** on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

## How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to **Subscriptions → Change Plan** in the billing portal.

## Where can I get my invoice?
Go to **Billing History** and click **Download PDF** next to any invoice.

## How do I contact support?
Call, WhatsApp, or email us — see the [Support Channels](../call-center/support-channels.md) page.
